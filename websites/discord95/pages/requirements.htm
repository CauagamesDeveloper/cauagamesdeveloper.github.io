Discord95 Requirements

As you know, Discord95 is a project made by thecauagames to bring Discord into older operating systems. But not all older computers supports Discord95.

Below there is the list of the minimum requirements to operate successfully Discord95.

Minimum Requirements

  • Processor: any processor with SSE4.1 support
  • RAM: 512MB
  • Storage: minimum of 8GB
  • Operating System: Windows 2000® SP4

Recommended Requirements

  • Processor: Pentium® 4 or equivalent AMD processor
  • RAM: 2GB
  • Storage: 20GB
  • Operating System: Windows XP® SP3
